Jebel Ali is the southern district of Dubai, south of Jumeirah and bordering the emirate of Abu Dhabi to the south.

Get in

[edit]
24°55′22″N 55°3′52″E
Map of Dubai/Jebel Ali

Jebel Ali is the end point of the red metro line and has three stations. Sheikh Zayed Road is the backbone and also serves as the road to Abu Dhabi.

Dubai World Central - Al Maktoum International Airport (DWC IATA) is Dubai's rapidly expanding second airport As of 2019, it was serving mainly flights from Russia, Saudi Arabia, India and Germany. Most flights land at Dubai International Airport in the Deira area.

See

[edit]
  • 24.976255.07321 Port of Jebel Ali. The world's largest man-made harbour and the biggest port in the Middle East. It was constructed in the late 1970s to supplement the facilities at Port Rashid. It is located thirty-five kilometres southwest of Dubai, in the Persian Gulf. On April 9, 2011 Jebel Ali Port won the Golden Award for Best Seaport Overall from the Higher Committee for UAE Civil Seaports and Airports Security.  (updated Oct 2020)

Do

[edit]
  • 24.96111155.0342371 Jebel Ali Shooting Club. This club has awesome architecture and design. It has wide range of shooting options to practice right from automatic ball trap, Olympic trap, down the line, Olympic skeet to compak sporting.  (updated Jun 2019)
  • Switch Bowling. A multi-lane bowling section, one of the perfect indoor entertainment venue for families and kids. In addition, it has billiards section, video gaming areas, toddlers' play area and restaurants. 
  • Peekaboo!. An ideal place for children below 7 years, for entertaining and creative activities. There is a variety of play equipment, toys and classes for kids. 
  • 24.98898955.0235432 Seawings. Experience live areal views and sightseeing excursions of Dubai's modern skyline and dazzling landmarks. The water-based take-offs and landings further adds greater experience. 1700 UAE.  
  • Jetty Lounge. This is an elegant beach lounge that has fantastic drinks. Cozy, but over-crowded during the sunsets. 
  • Oasis Town of Dahid. Explore the village, its traditions and the oasis. 
  • 24.92075855.0103913 Dubai Parks & Resorts (Exit 5 off E11 Highway (Sheikh Zayed Road)). 11AM to 7PM. Dubai Parks and Resorts is the Middle East's largest integrated leisure and theme park destination featuring more than 100 rides and attractions, and consists of three theme parks: Motiongate Dubai, Bollywood Parks Dubai, and Legoland Dubai, and one water park: Legoland Water Park. It also encompasses Riverland Dubai, which is a themed retail and dining destination, as well as the Polynesian-themed family resort, Lapita Hotel Dubai.  (updated Jul 2021)
